Starting ten years ago, massive open online course platforms or एमओओसी प्लेटफॉर्म began releasing online degree programs in partnership with universities. This trend started slowly, butगति पकड़ रहा है.

The first MOOC-based degree, the कंप्यूटर विज्ञान में ऑनलाइन मास्टर ऑफ साइंस (ओएमएससीएस) from जॉर्जिया टेक via उधम, was announced back in 2013. It took two years for the next one (the आईएमबीए from the University of Illinois via Coursera) to be announced in 2015. By early 2017, at least 9 master’s degrees were available through एमओओसी प्लेटफॉर्म. Then in 2018, things really took off. Now, beginning 2022, there are no fewer than 70 degrees you can complete online, through a एमओओसी प्लेटफॉर्म.

क्लास सेंट्रल also compiled online degrees available in India and China.

एमओओसी आधारित डिग्री बनाम पारंपरिक डिग्री

  • MOOC-based degrees offer a number of advantages, including lower cost compared to other online and on-campus degrees, flexibility, and pay-as-you-go pricing. क्लास सेंट्रल’s धवल शाह summarized the differences between MOOC-based degrees and other kinds of online degrees inएडसर्ज के लिए एक लेख.
  • Another benefit for both prospective students and learners with no intention of enrolling in the full degree program is that many courses which are part of the degree are free to take. That means you can preview parts of the content before committing to anything (A full list of MOOCs that are part of degree programs and other for-credit offerings can be found on क्लास सेंट्रल here).
  • It is also worth noting that all of these degrees are fully accredited and recognized. In most cases, the final degree doesn’t in any way indicate that the credential was earned online.

कुल मिलाकर इन डिग्रियों को देखते हुए, यहाँ कुछ सारांश आँकड़े दिए गए हैं:

  • Platforms: Among एमओओसी प्लेटफॉर्म, Coursera leads the pack with 34 degree programs. फ्यूचरलर्न is close behind with 24 degree programs. एडएक्स currently offers 13 मास्टर डिग्री. उधम continues to offer one legacy degree program, the ओएमएससीएस from जॉर्जिया टेक, which was the first MOOC-based degree ever to be offered.
  • Costs: Oftentimes, MOOC-based degrees are cheaper than both on-campus and online alternatives. (Both the ओएमएससीएस from जॉर्जिया टेक and the आईएमबीए are good examples of the cost savings.) However, most MOOC-based degrees still run between $10,000 and $22,000, and some cost more than that. The most expensive MOOC-based master’s degree is the सार्वजनिक स्वास्थ्य के मास्टर from the मिशिगन यूनिवर्सिटी on Coursera.
  • Type of degree: Most MOOC-based degrees are master’s degrees. However, there are now 7 MOOC-based bachelor’s degrees, 5 degrees offered by Coursera and 2 by फ्यूचरलर्न.
  • Trial courses: All the online degree programs on this list offer ways to try-before-you-buy (or try-before-you-apply). These are labeled differently on the different platforms (e.g. taster courses on फ्यूचरलर्न, Specializations on Coursera), but all essentially consist of MOOC coursework you can complete for free or at low cost before enrolling in a degree program.

कंप्यूटर विज्ञान और इंजीनियरिंग मास्टर डिग्री

Announced with much fanfare in 2013, जॉर्जिया टेक’s ओएमएससीएस through उधम was the first degree you could earn on a एमओओसी प्लेटफॉर्म. The tuition fee has since then increased from$402 प्रति तीन-क्रेडिट घंटे to$540, but it is still one of the most cost effective (and most popular) master’s degrees in computer science on the market. Enrollment for fall 2020 is 2,890 छात्र, making the ओएमएससीएस one of the largest computer science degree programs in the world.

For several years, the ओएमएससीएस was the only degree of its kind on the market. However, there are now 11 MOOC-based master’s degrees in computer science. Launched in 2018, the कंप्यूटर और सूचना प्रौद्योगिकी के मास्टर from the University of Pennsylvania’s and Coursera is the first MOOC-based degree to be offered by an Ivy League university. Another first-of-its-kind degree, the इलेक्ट्रिकल इंजीनियरिंग में मास्टर ऑफ साइंस from the University of Colorado, Boulder, was announced in 2018.
