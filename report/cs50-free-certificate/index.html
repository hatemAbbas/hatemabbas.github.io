2023 में हार्वर्ड CS50: निःशुल्क प्रमाणपत्र कैसे प्राप्त करें

हार्वर्ड’s intro to computer science was just updated! Here’s what’s new and how to earn a free certificate.

मनोबल Cortes Mendez Jan 9th, 2023
  • फेसबुक
  • ट्विटर
  • लिफ़ाफ़ा
  • 65

With its over 4.3 million enrollments, CS50, हार्वर्ड’s मैंntroduction to कंप्यूटर विज्ञान, is one of the most popular online courses ever. And it’s one of क्लास सेंट्रल‘s अब तक का सर्वश्रेष्ठ ऑनलाइन पाठ्यक्रम.

Having taken the course myself, मैं can’t say मैं’m surprised. The course is excellent: it has a fantastic instructor; it offers a rich learning experience; and the course content is refreshed every year, on January 1st.

Most notably, the course is entirely free, and it includes a पूरा होने का मुफ़्त प्रमाण पत्र. But understanding how to obtain it can be a bit confusing. So in this article, let’s discuss CS50 and explain how you can earn a free certificate.

  • What’s New in 2023
  • विभिन्न प्रमाणीकरण प्रकार
  • फ्री अकाउंट कैसे प्राप्त करें

(हार्वर्ड also offers other free courses on topics such as Python, web development, and Aमैं. Learn more in our हार्वर्ड CS50 गाइड.)

CS50 अवलोकन

CS50 is taught by हार्वर्ड Professor डेविड जे मालन, who’s also been teaching the course on campus since 2007. Malan is a real showman. Forget dull PowerPoint presentations with monotone voice-overs. Malan takes the stage in हार्वर्ड’s beautiful सैंडर्स थिएटर to enthusiastically teach computer science in front of a live audience.

मैंt’s one of the most striking aspects of the course. मैंt’s really well made. Malan paces the stage while filmed from multiple angles, using props to explain concepts, inviting students on stage for interactive demos, or standing at his laptop in front of a green screen for live coding sessions.

The course production values are off the charts, and Malan’s passion for teaching computer science is palpable. Naturally, this has contributed to the course’s success. Even by campus standards, CS50 stands out. With around 1,000 students enrolled every Fall, CS50 is the largest course on हार्वर्ड’s campus.

मुझे लगता है कि ऑनलाइन कोर्स की मुख्य ताकतें यह हैं कि मालन इसे अपने ऑन-कैंपस कोर्स की तरह ही मानते हैं। ऑनलाइन और ऑन-कैंपस पाठ्यक्रम एक और समान हैं, इसके बजाय पूर्व के संस्करण का कम संस्करण होगा। दोनों के पास एक ही व्याख्यान, एक ही समस्या सेट और एक ही अंतिम परियोजना है। सीखने के मामले में, यह वास्तव में पूरे हार्वर्ड के अनुभव के करीब है।

What’s New in 2023

Another strength of CS50 is that the course is updated every year, allowing it to keep up with the latest trends in the field. More specifically, the course is recorded every Fall on campus at हार्वर्ड. Then, come January 1st, the online version is updated to use the new recordings. For instance, CS50 2023 uses the recordings made at हार्वर्ड in Fall 2022.

मैंn my experience, annual recordings are exceptionally rare in online education. मैंn the vast majority of cases, lectures are recorded once and reused throughout the lifespan of the course. The only other course मैं can think of that has annual recordings is MमैंT’s मैंntroduction to Deep Learning. This seems to be a privilege restricted to a few, popular courses at rich institutions.

वार्षिक रिकॉर्डिंग भी पाठ्यक्रम अपडेट के लिए एक अवसर है। 2023 में, CS50 ने दूसरों के बीच निम्नलिखित बदलाव किए:

  • Practice Problems: Every week, before tackling the lab and problem set, you’ll now be able to complete optional practice problems to cement your understanding of the week’s lesson. For instance, here are the पहले चरण का जोखिमभंडार.
  • More Labs: मैंn Week 1, the जनसंख्या वृद्धि lab, present 2021 में but absent 2022 में, has returned. And an entirely new lab, स्माइली, has been added to Week 4, where you’ll learn to manipulate images at the pixel level.
  • More Problems: Last but not least, new problems have been added to problem sets. मैंn Week 2, you’ll be able to complete the following new problems:
    • B, an entry-level problem, where you’ll practice binary encoding.
    • Wordle50, a more challenging problem, where you’ll write a program to play the popular word game Wordle.

Like in previous years, CS50 ends with an open-ended सॉफ्टवेयर परियोजना ⁠— an opportunity for students to put into practice what they’ve learned throughout the course.

विभिन्न प्रमाणीकरण प्रकार

So, you think this course is for you, and you’d like to get started. Great! But before, there’s one thing worth clarifying, because it often confuses learners. CS50 is offered on four platforms, but only one offers a free certificate.

❌ These three platforms offer CS50, but they do not include a पूरा होने का मुफ़्त प्रमाण पत्र:

  • एडएक्स, which is the platform most people are usually familiar with. On एडएक्स, you can audit CS50 for free. However, एडएक्स doesn’t offer a free certificate. मैंt only offers a भुगतान पक्का प्रमाण पत्र, which costs $149.
  • हार्वर्ड स्कूल, which is part of हार्वर्ड’s distance education division. Through this school, CS50 doesn’t include a free certificate. मैंnstead, students can take the course for credit and receive a formal transcript, which costs $1980–3100.
  • हार्वर्ड स्कूल समर, which is also part of हार्वर्ड’s distance education division. Though this school, CS50 doesn’t include a free certificate either. मैंnstead, students can take the course for credit and receive a formal transcript, which costs $3500.

✔️ This platform offers CS50, and it does include a पूरा होने का मुफ़्त प्रमाण पत्र:

  • हार्वर्ड ओडब्ल्यू, which is हार्वर्ड’s open online course platform. On हार्वर्ड ओडब्ल्यू, CS50 includes a पूरा होने का मुफ़्त प्रमाण पत्र, like the one below. Note that the course content is exactly same as on एडएक्स, including all the assignments. The केवल अंतर is that the free certificate doesn’t involve मैंD verification.

Finally, to further confuse matters, even when you take the course via हार्वर्ड ओडब्ल्यू, you’ll be asked to create a free एडएक्स account. To be clear, this is सिर्फ एक तारक उपाय that will allow you to submit your assignments. You do not need to buy the एडएक्स verified certificate.

फ्री अकाउंट कैसे प्राप्त करें

Since its launch, CS50 has maintained a firm stance toward openness through the हार्वर्ड ओडब्ल्यू platform. मैंn 2023, the course remains entirely free on the platform, including its समाप्ति का प्रमाण पत्र. Above, you can see how the free certificate looks.

To प्रमाणीकरण अनलॉक करें, you have to score 70% or more on:

  1. All the labs. There are 10 labs, one per week.
  2. All the problems sets. There are also 10 problems sets, one per week.
  3. The final project, in the last week.

Note that you can resubmit assignments. So if you don’t pass on the first try, no problem. You can try again.

Ready? Here’s where to get started: हार्वर्ड CS50 — Week 0: वीडियो सब & समस्या सेट. Then, simply go week by week. Happy learning!
